Auditing Services

Audit & Assurance

At Anil Pol & Associates, our audit process is designed to provide high-level assurance to stakeholders. We go beyond the numbers to identify risks and provide insights that help improve your business operations.

Our Expertise Includes:

  • Statutory Audits: Ensuring compliance with Companies Act and legal requirements.
  • Internal Audits: Evaluating internal controls and operational efficiency.
  • Tax Audits: Conducting mandatory audits under the Income Tax Act.
  • GST Audits: Verifying records for indirect tax compliance.
  • Due Diligence: Providing detailed financial reviews for business transactions.

The Value of Our Audit

Objectivity

Independent and unbiased verification of your financial records.

Risk Mitigation

Identifying potential financial discrepancies before they become legal issues.

Business Insights

Constructive feedback to strengthen your financial reporting framework.
